Frequently Asked Questions

How do I import a GPX file?

Open ReRun, tap Import, and select a .gpx file from your device. You can export GPX files from apps like Strava, Garmin Connect, or Apple Fitness.

Where is my data stored?

All processing happens entirely on your device. Your GPX files are never uploaded to any server.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

Open ReRun, go to Settings, and tap Restore Purchases. Your Pro status will be restored if you have an active subscription or lifetime purchase on your Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings on your device, tap Apple ID, then Subscriptions, find ReRun Pro, and tap Cancel. Your Pro access will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

The app is not opening my GPX file.

ReRun supports standard GPX 1.1 files with trackpoint data. If your file was exported from an unusual source, it may not be in a supported format. Email us the file and we will investigate.
